29 primary regulator

A regulator with administrative privileges for the regulator account

 Example: A city might have the city council assigned as the primary regulator. The city council could then establish the police department and traffic department as additional regulators for the same jurisdiction. All of the regulators would have the authority to establish rules while only the city council would have the authority to designate additional regulators.
